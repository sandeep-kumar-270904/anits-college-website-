Our department blends strong fundamentals with a focus on sustainability, process intensification, and emerging technologies to prepare future-ready engineers, researchers, and leaders. We offer a B.Tech program in Chemical Engineering with both regular and honors tracks, along with options for a Minor degree from other departments and a PG program in Food Processing Technology. Our curriculum emphasizes core concepts, hands-on experience through advanced labs, and real-world problem solving. Chemical engineers from our department are trained to design processes that convert raw materials into valuable products, guided by principles of safety, economics, and environmental responsibility. Our faculty are dedicated mentors and active researchers who help students excel in sectors like petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and academia. We promote holistic development through co-curricular and extracurricular activities such as IIChE chapters, technical symposiums, workshops, cultural events, and the RACE club. (WK8). (WK8) PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)- CSE (DATA SCIENCE) PEO 1 Graduates will possess strong technical skills in Data Science and related fields, applying innovative solutions to real-world challenges. PEO 2 Graduates will engage in research, contribute to the advancements of Data Science, and continuously update their knowledge to stay aligned with emerging technologies. PEO 3 Graduates will be equipped for successful careers in Data Science, gaining practical experience through internships and collaborative industry projects, with a strong commitment to ethical practices in addressing global challenges. PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSOs) – CSE (DATA SCIENCE) PSO 1 Apply suitable statistical models, tools and techniques to perform data analysis and visualization for effective decision making. PSO 2 Apply standard and advanced data science concepts, practices, and strategies to develop sustainable products that leverage data science techniques for addressing prevalent societal problems.
